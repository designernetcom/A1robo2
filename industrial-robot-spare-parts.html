Industrial Robot Spare Parts

Industrial robots are at the core of modern automated manufacturing, delivering precision, consistency, and speed across a wide range of industries including automotive, electronics, pharmaceuticals, packaging, and heavy machinery. However, like any complex machinery, these robots require regular maintenance and timely replacement of components to ensure optimal performance. Even a small malfunction can lead to costly downtime, production losses, and compromised quality. That’s where the availability of high-quality spare parts becomes essential.

Our company specializes in providing a complete range of industrial robot spare parts, supporting some of the most trusted and widely used brands in the automation industry—FANUC, ABB, KUKA, and YASKAWA. We maintain an extensive inventory that includes both genuine OEM parts and high-grade compatible alternatives, offering flexible options to suit different budgets and operational needs.

For FANUC robots, we stock a wide array of components including R-J3 to R-30iB controllers, servo motors, iPendant units, amplifiers, power modules, brake units, encoders, and complete wiring harnesses. Similarly, our ABB range covers circuit breakers, servo drives, contactors, FlexPendant interfaces, robotware modules, and cabling systems, all built for reliability in high-load environments

Our KUKA spare part lineup features KRC controllers, smartPADs, gearboxes, cooling fans, heat exchangers, and robot arm joints designed for precision movement and longevity. For YASKAWA/Motoman systems, we offer servo amplifiers, motor encoders, DX and YRC series controllers, cables, and custom I/O solutions—all essential for keeping advanced motion systems functioning smoothly.

Every part we supply undergoes rigorous quality checks, and many are available in both new and refurbished conditions, offering flexibility depending on your maintenance strategy and budget. Refurbished parts are restored to near-original condition and tested to ensure full operational integrity.
